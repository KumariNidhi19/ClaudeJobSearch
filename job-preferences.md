# Job Preferences

> These are the hard rules Claude applies when ranking and filtering jobs.
> Edit freely — the automation reads this file verbatim every run.

## Roles I want
Product Designer, Senior Product Designer, UI/UX Designer, UX Designer,
AI Product Designer, Design Lead (individual contributor). Priority to roles in
**GenAI / applied AI, enterprise SaaS, B2B/B2C SaaS, pharma/life-sciences,
healthcare**.

## Location & geography
- **Open to all locations.** **Pune is preferred**, but do not filter out any
  city, state, or country on location alone.
- Remote and hybrid roles anywhere are welcome (India-based or global remote).
- Rank order when fit is otherwise equal: Pune > other Indian metros / remote >
  rest. Location is a soft preference — the schedule rules below outrank it.

## Experience band
Mid to senior IC, roughly **3–6 years**. Skip roles demanding 8+ years or
people-management as the core requirement.

---

## Work-schedule rules (STRICT — this is the primary filter)

**Priority order — rank every job by this:**

1. **Highest priority — Hybrid, 2–3 office days/week.** Roles requiring office
   attendance only 2–3 days per week.
2. **Second — Standard 5-day work week.** Onsite, hybrid, or fully remote, as
   long as it is a normal 5-day week.
3. **Reject — 6-day work weeks and alternate-Saturday schedules** (e.g. 1st &
   3rd Saturdays working, or any "6 days" / "alternate Saturdays off" pattern).

**Exception to rule 3:** A 6-day or alternate-Saturday role is allowed **only**
if the employer is a top-tier, globally recognized organization of the caliber
of **BCG, JPMorgan Chase, PwC, Deloitte, Capgemini**, or comparable in scale,
prestige and career value (e.g. McKinsey, Bain, EY, KPMG, Accenture, Google,
Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, and similar). For every
other employer, a 6-day or alternate-Saturday schedule means **reject**.

**When the schedule is not stated** in the posting: do not assume. Mark the
schedule as `unknown`, still include the job, and flag it clearly so I can
verify before applying. Do not let an unknown schedule outrank a confirmed
2–3 day hybrid role.

## Nice-to-have (boosts ranking, never required)
- Strong design-system / component-library culture.
- AI-first or research-driven product teams.
- Companies building copilots, assistants, or human-in-the-loop tooling.

## Hard nos
- Pure graphic-design / marketing-creative-only roles.
- Unpaid, "equity-only", or internship postings.
- Roles that are 90%+ visual/branding with no product UX.
- Agencies pitching client-servicing churn with no product ownership (unless
  schedule + comp are clearly strong).
